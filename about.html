GET TO KNOW US

Lazeeza serves Louisville the best halal food for everyone to enjoy.

Our Mission

Lazeeza's mission is to bring traditional home-cooked meals from the Middle East straight to your table. We want to make getting halal food accessible and affordable as well as giving others a chance for a cultural dining experience they will never forget. Lazeeza wants every person of any background to have the chance to enjoy these timeless meals.

Meet the Owner

Hussein Ahmed is the founder and owner of Louisville's Lazeeza Halal Restaurant. He has dedicated over 15 years of his life to Lazeeza. His goal is to bring his home meals to your table.
